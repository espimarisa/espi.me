Hi, my name's Espi!

I'm a blind developer, tinkerer, and activist from Huntsville, Alabama.

Currently, I'm a final year Computer Science student at Auburn University.


I work with the Alabama Transgender Rights Action Coalition to fight discriminatory legislature.

In addition, I help maintain a collection of local resources for queer and transgender Alabamians.


In my spare time, I enjoy watching anime, researching severe weather, playing the guitar (poorly), anything Pokémon related, and exploring my home region of Appalachia and the further deep south.
