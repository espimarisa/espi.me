About

I'm a visually impaired nonbinary gal proudly from Northeast Tennessee who now calls North Alabama home. Currently, I'm an IT and Political Science dual-degree student at The University of North Alabama.

I built my first desktop computer before I knew how to speak properly, and that hobby has stuck with me for my entire life. I like messing with enthusiast PC hardware and enjoy overclocking anything that I possibly can.

In my spare time, I work with ALTRAC (The Alabama Transgender Rights Action Coalition), fighting against discriminatory legislature introduced by the Alabama state house, and pushing for proper representation.

Outside of work, I enjoy listening to music, playing video games, reading, thrifting, and watching anime.
